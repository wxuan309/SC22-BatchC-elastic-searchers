Find Out More

Why Care?

Currently, climate change is a global problem in which everyone's awareness of their own environmental impact is greatly important. The world's transportation sector accounts for 37% of CO2 emissions from end-use sectors, making a significant part of our awareness needing to be on transportation. We created this product so that individuals can easily have a better understanding of their own car's CO2 emissions in order to become a more informed consumer and individual as we all try and reduce our environmental impacts.

Our Story


  • Week 1

    In the first week, we started off learning the basics of python and machine learning. We looked at multiple data sets before settling down on the CO2 emissions data set.

  • Week 2

    In the second week, we learned and experimented with several machine learning models on our CO2 emissions data set. We tried several machine learning models such as XG boost, Random Forest, Lasso Linear Regression, Support Vector Regression, and Ridge Regression finding that the Lasso Linear Regression delivered the best score out of all these machine learning models. We also made beautiful visualizations of these machine learning models.

  • Week 3

    In the third week, we learned html basics and we implemented our findings and everything we learned in the past three weeks into one website.

Conclusion

After trying various machine learning models and determining their NRMSE(normalized root mean square error), we determined that the lasso linear regression model would work best for our data. We then went on to narrow down potential inputs, keeping what the user would know about their car in mind. You can now input details about your own vehicle below in order to use our model and get an estimate of your vehicle's CO2 emissions. We hope that with this information about your own impact, you will be a more informed consumer when shopping for new vehicles and when using your current one.
